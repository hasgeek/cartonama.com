Hangout with Bill Morris 12 September 2012

Join Bill Morris and other map data enthusiasts in a Google Hangout tonight at 7:30pm IST, 28 August 2012.

Bill is principal at GeoSprocket LLC, a multifaceted geospatial consulting firm. With over a decade of experience in geographic information science, remote sensing and cartography, he focuses on applying the strength of spatial relationships to real-world challenges in the areas of environmental resource conservation, agricultural management and international development.

Join the Hangout directly and post your questions in live chat or if you don't have a Google+ account you can watch the Hangout live on YouTube.
