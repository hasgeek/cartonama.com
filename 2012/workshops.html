Cartonama Workshops

We’ve scheduled the hands-on workshops for Cartonama on separate days following the main event.

When: September 23, 24 & 25

Where: The Centre for Internet and Society, No. 194, Second 'C' Cross, Domlur,  2nd Stage, Bangalore

The workshops require a separate ticket from the main conference and have a limited capacity. Please use the form on the right to purchase a ticket for them.

Participants are also required to bring their laptops for the workshops. The schedule is below.

Sunday, September 23 - Geo-simulation using open Geodata
10:00am-12:00pm 

Sunday, September 23 - Reading and Writing GeoData on the Web
2:00pm-5:00pm 

Monday, September 24 - GeoCommons and TileMill: Stories through Maps
9:30am-12:30pm 

Tuesday, September 25 - Sourcing, processing, and indexing location data for search
9:30am - 12:30pm
